Equipment for MakeFest

This page provides information on the equipment that MakeFest participants should have.

Minimal requirements

  • a computer (ideally with Windows, MacOS also works)
  • 20 GB of free storage
  • 4 GB of memory
  • a webcam
  • a microphone, ideally as part of a headset
  • an internet connection with upstream and downstream speed of at least 1 Mbit (test with nperf.com)
