Advent Calendar Game

For about 2 years now, six MakeFest participants are developing an Advent Calendar Game.

The game has 24 levels, one for each day from December 1st to 24th.

And each level is a mini-game that tells one chapter of a Christmas story.

Below you can find a video and a playable version of part of a level.
