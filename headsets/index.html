Bluetooth headsets

Bluetooth headsets can be nice and of course there are headsets that offer better audio quality. But good bluetooth headsets are expensive, starting at around 100 CHF and easily going beyond 200 CHF, see list below.

I am not advising against them but in my experience it is in any case very helpful to have a decent USB headset available.

Headphones

An even cheaper option is to use a headphone without a microphone. In that case one would use another microphone for video-conferencing, for instance one that is integrated with the webcam.

Simple and sufficient in-ear or on-ear headphones can be found for a little over 10 CHF, for example:

  • JBL T110
  • JVC HA-L50
